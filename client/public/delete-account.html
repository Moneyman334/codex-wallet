Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to request deletion of your GetCodexPay account and what data will be removed.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the GetCodexPay app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon in the bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Account Settings"
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your identity by entering your password
  6. Tap "Permanently Delete Account" to confirm

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: support@getcodexpay.com

Please include the email address associated with your account in your deletion request. We will process your request within 30 days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Transaction history within our platform
  • Saved preferences and settings
  • Connected wallet addresses (association only, not the wallets themselves)
  • Any stored payment methods

Data That May Be Retained

  • Transaction records required for legal/regulatory compliance (up to 7 years)
  • Data necessary to prevent fraud or enforce our terms of service
  • Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you

Important Warning

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Before deleting your account:

  • Withdraw any remaining cryptocurrency balances to an external wallet
  • Export any transaction records you may need
  • Cancel any active subscriptions or pending transactions

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data privacy, please contact us at support@getcodexpay.com
